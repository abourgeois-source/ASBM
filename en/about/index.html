About ASBM

You shouldn't need more people to fix work
that should already run smoothly

ASBM builds practical AI-powered systems that eliminate manual work, reduce errors, and give your operations more control.

The real problem
When manual work piles up, growth pays the price.

When your team spends hours on repetitive tasks, correcting avoidable mistakes, or moving information between disconnected systems, growth becomes slower, more expensive, and harder to control.

That's where the real problem starts. Not in the task itself. In the time, energy, and consistency it quietly drains from your business.

ASBM helps you reduce workflow errors and eliminate repetitive manual work by building practical AI-powered operational systems.

Not complicated technology. Not automation for the sake of automation. Systems that make daily operations clearer, faster, and more reliable.

Whether it's internal processes, client follow-up, reporting, admin workflows, or team coordination, the goal stays the same: a business that runs with less friction and more control.

You gain
  • Fewer manual mistakes across your daily operations.
  • Less time lost on tasks your team shouldn't be doing by hand.
  • Better visibility across every part of your business.
  • Smoother workflows that don't depend on one person to hold them together.
  • More space to focus on what actually grows the business.

Efficiency isn't about doing more. It's about removing what should never have required your attention in the first place.

The founder
Aljosja Bourgeois

Before starting ASBM, Aljosja worked as a freelance business consultant, mainly improving sales and marketing operations. He spent years working with business owners who were smart, motivated, and completely buried in operational work that had nothing to do with growth. The same manual tasks. The same disconnected tools. The same hours lost to admin that should have been automated long ago.

He started ASBM to fix that. Not with generic software or templates, but by working directly with each business to find the real bottlenecks and build automations that fit how they actually operate.

The approach is straightforward: the consulting and strategy side is handled directly with you. The technical build is taken care of by a skilled delivery team. You get fast, high-quality implementation without the overhead costs of a large agency.

The work is practical, the communication is direct, and the goal is always the same: get the manual work out of your day so you can focus on what actually matters.

The process
How it works with clients
01

Diagnose

You find out where your time and money are actually going. In one conversation, the processes costing you the most get identified and prioritised.

02

Design

You see exactly what gets automated and how. No jargon. No technical decisions for you to make. Just a clear picture of what gets built and what it fixes.

03

Build

The automation gets built inside the tools you already use. You stay informed without having to manage the project yourself.

04

Hand over

The automation goes live. You get a full walkthrough and the support to make sure it keeps running correctly after delivery.

Who this is for
Growing businesses that want practical results, not unnecessary complexity.

ASBM works with small and medium businesses across Europe. The common thread isn't the industry or the size. It's that operations are eating time that should be going into growth.

  • You're spending 10 or more hours a week on repetitive tasks that could run automatically.
  • You want to take on more clients or volume but your current process doesn't scale without hiring more people.
  • You've tried tools like Zapier or Make but couldn't get them to work the way your business actually runs.
  • You know something needs to change but you're not sure what to automate first or how to approach it.
